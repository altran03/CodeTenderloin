All About Whales

Learn more about whales at National Geographic Whale.

Whale Facts

  1. Whales are the largest animals on Earth.
  2. Some whales, like the blue whale, can reach lengths of over 100 feet.
  3. Even though they live in the ocean, whales are mammals, just like us, and must come to the surface to breathe air.
  4. They communicate using complex sounds and songs.
  5. Some species migrate thousands of miles each year.

What Do Whales Eat?

  • Krill
  • Small fish
  • Squid
