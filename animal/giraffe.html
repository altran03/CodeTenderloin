Giraffe Facts

  1. Giraffes are the tallest land animals, able to reach up to 18 feet in height.
  2. They have long necks with only seven vertebrae (bone), just like humans.
  3. Giraffes' tongues can be up to 18 inches long and are dark-colored to prevent sunburn.
  4. Each giraffe has a unique pattern of spots.
  5. Giraffes can run at speeds up to 35 miles per hour over short distances despite their size.

What Do Giraffes Eat?

  • Acacia leaves
  • Twigs
  • Fruits
