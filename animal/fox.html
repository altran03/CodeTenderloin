Fox Facts

  1. Foxes are members of the dog family, Canidae.
  2. They have excellent hearing and can hear rodents digging underground.
  3. Foxes use their bushy tails for warmth and balance.
  4. There are over 30 species of foxes worldwide.
  5. Foxes are known for their intelligence and adaptability.

What Do Foxes Eat?

  • Birds
  • Fruits
  • Insects
